The objective of the game is to decipher the computer's secret combination of colors in the fewest number of attempts possible.
In each turn, you have to select a combination of colors, which is presented as a row on the board.
After each attempt, check to see if the combination matches the winning combination or not.
The computer will mark the colors that are in the correct position with purple, and the colors that are in the incorrect position with white.
The game ends when the player correctly guesses the secret combination or when they run out of attempts.
